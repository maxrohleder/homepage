The Machine Learning Project

In early 2017, I got interested in Artificial Intelligence, so started reading into the topic. After learning all the basics about Neural Networks and working through the math behind them, I wanted to implement something to really get a feeling for it. In this project I will give an introduction to all the ideas necessary to understand Neural Networks and actually implement a basic NN!

Neural Networks were actually inspired by the way the brain works.
